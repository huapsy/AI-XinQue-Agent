# 要点 56：DECATASTROPHIZING

## 简介

要点 56：DECATASTROPHIZING。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「This is taking the ‘horror’ (emotional disturbance) out of a feared event by encouraging clients to see themselves dealing adaptively with their catastrophic predictions and reminding themselves that assuming the worst is, by definition, an unlikely outcome. Catastrophic thinking is often generated by ‘What if …?’ statements such as ‘What if I panic in the supermarket?’ or ‘What if I fail my exams?’ (see Point 39). Barlow and Cerny (1988: 132)…」

## 步骤

1. 选定一个最近发生的、足够小的具体情境。
2. 按技术步骤练习，并记录情绪强度（0–10）与关键想法。
3. 写下练习后的新发现（哪怕很小）。
4. 若痛苦骤升，暂停并改用稳定化策略或寻求支持。

## 应用场景与问题

**常见困扰**

- 自动化思维、回避、焦虑抑郁相关模式

**相关情绪与症状**

- 焦虑、抑郁、羞耻、愤怒

**典型情境**

- 可具体想象的生活场景
