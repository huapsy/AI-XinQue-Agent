# 要点 26：SETTING THE AGENDA

## 简介

要点 26：SETTING THE AGENDA。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「An agenda is a short list of items that will be the focus of that particular session. Setting an agenda is deemed by CBT therapists to be the best way of making the optimum use of the therapy time (though clients who have been in non- directive forms of therapy may become irritated at what they see as restrictions on their freedom to wander mentally; the therapist may need to explain patiently and repeatedly the rationale for agenda setting). …」

## 步骤

1. 准备 10–15 分钟与纸笔或文档。
2. 按条目要求逐项填写（问题、目标、议程等）。
3. 标出本周最重要的一条，并写一个可执行的微行动。
4. 回顾时调整计划，不自我攻击。

## 应用场景与问题

**常见困扰**

- 生活混乱、需梳理问题与目标

**相关情绪与症状**

- 无助、拖延、混乱感

**典型情境**

- 周初计划、睡前整理、咨询间隙

## 适用与禁忌

- 极度绝望时先联系信任的人或热线。
