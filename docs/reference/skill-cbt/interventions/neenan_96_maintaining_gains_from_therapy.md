# 要点 96：MAINTAINING GAINS FROM THERAPY

## 简介

要点 96：MAINTAINING GAINS FROM THERAPY。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「Achieving one’s goals is not the same process as maintaining them (e.g. getting fit, then staying fit; losing weight, then keeping to the new weight). Some clients might believe that once therapy is terminated their therapeutic gains will ‘magic-ally’ stay intact without any further input from them, that they deserve a prolonged rest after all their hard work in therapy, or that therapy was a discrete, crisis- driven episode that they now can …」

## 步骤

1. 选定一个最近发生的、足够小的具体情境。
2. 按技术步骤练习，并记录情绪强度（0–10）与关键想法。
3. 写下练习后的新发现（哪怕很小）。
4. 若痛苦骤升，暂停并改用稳定化策略或寻求支持。
