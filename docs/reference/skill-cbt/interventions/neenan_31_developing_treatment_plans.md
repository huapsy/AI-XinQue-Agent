# 要点 31：DEVELOPING TREATMENT PLANS

## 简介

要点 31：DEVELOPING TREATMENT PLANS。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「A treatment plan is derived from the client’s case conceptualiza-tion and provides an overall view of how the client can free him-self from the ‘grip’ of the disorder and achieve his therapy goals. The treatment plan is composed of a series of interventions that will modify those factors maintaining the client’s disorder (if a client objects to the term ‘treatment plan’ because it sounds too medical, the therapist can substitute ‘action plan’)…」

## 步骤

1. 准备 10–15 分钟与纸笔或文档。
2. 按条目要求逐项填写（问题、目标、议程等）。
3. 标出本周最重要的一条，并写一个可执行的微行动。
4. 回顾时调整计划，不自我攻击。

## 应用场景与问题

**常见困扰**

- 生活混乱、需梳理问题与目标

**相关情绪与症状**

- 无助、拖延、混乱感

**典型情境**

- 周初计划、睡前整理、咨询间隙

## 适用与禁忌

- 极度绝望时先联系信任的人或热线。
