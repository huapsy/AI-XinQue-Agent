# 要点 90：HISTORICAL TEST OF THE NEW CORE BELIEF

## 简介

要点 90：HISTORICAL TEST OF THE NEW CORE BELIEF。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「Maladaptive core beliefs are usually formed in childhood and the client may have collected a lot of evidence over the years to support and strengthen them. However, this evidence, which demonstrates the ‘truth’ about herself and her life, is a product of the consistently negative bias generated by these beliefs. Undertaking a retrospective survey of the client’s life viewed through the lens of the new core belief can be for her both productive…」

## 步骤

1. 选定一个最近发生的、足够小的具体情境。
2. 按技术步骤练习，并记录情绪强度（0–10）与关键想法。
3. 写下练习后的新发现（哪怕很小）。
4. 若痛苦骤升，暂停并改用稳定化策略或寻求支持。
