# 要点 57：EXPLORING DOUBLE STANDARDS

## 简介

要点 57：EXPLORING DOUBLE STANDARDS。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「Some clients apply a standard much more strictly to themselves than they do to others. For example, a mother condemned her-self as bad when her child fell over and injured himself while out playing (‘I can’t be any good as a mother for letting this happen’), but has words of sympathy and understanding, not condemnation, for a friend whose child suffered a similar mis-fortune (‘It can’t be helped that children injure themselves when they’re out…」

## 步骤

1. 选定一个最近发生的、足够小的具体情境。
2. 按技术步骤练习，并记录情绪强度（0–10）与关键想法。
3. 写下练习后的新发现（哪怕很小）。
4. 若痛苦骤升，暂停并改用稳定化策略或寻求支持。

## 应用场景与问题

**常见困扰**

- 自动化思维、回避、焦虑抑郁相关模式

**相关情绪与症状**

- 焦虑、抑郁、羞耻、愤怒

**典型情境**

- 可具体想象的生活场景

## 适用与禁忌

- 暴露与症状诱发类请在安全前提下进行。
