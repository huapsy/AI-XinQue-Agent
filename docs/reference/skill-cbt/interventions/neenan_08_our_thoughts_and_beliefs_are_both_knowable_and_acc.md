# 要点 8：OUR THOUGHTS AND BELIEFS ARE BOTH KNOWABLE AND ACCESSIBL…

## 简介

要点 8：OUR THOUGHTS AND BELIEFS ARE BOTH KNOWABLE AND ACCESSIBL…。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「Between an external stimulus (e.g. being criticized) and an emotional response to it (e.g. anger) lie a person’s thoughts about this event. Eliciting these thoughts helps the person to understand why she reacted in the way that she did to the event. Beck calls this ‘tapping the internal communications’ and states that clients can be trained ‘to focus on their introspections [examining one’s thoughts] in various situations. The person can then …」

## 步骤

1. 用一两句话写下触发事件（事实，不带评价）。
2. 分别记下：想法、情绪名称、身体感受、外显行为（能写多少写多少）。
3. 问自己：这些反应如何互相加强？改变其中一环可能带来什么不同？
4. 情绪激动时只做记录，不要求当场「想通」。

## 应用场景与问题

**常见困扰**

- 困扰反复、情绪波动、想理解「怎么了」

**相关情绪与症状**

- 焦虑、低落、愤怒、紧张、失眠伴反刍

**典型情境**

- 工作、家庭、人际、健康事件

## 适用与禁忌

- 危机时以安全为先。
