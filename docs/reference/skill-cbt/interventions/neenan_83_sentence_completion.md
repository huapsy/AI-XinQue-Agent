# 要点 83：SENTENCE COMPLETION

## 简介

要点 83：SENTENCE COMPLETION。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「As core beliefs are usually stated in global terms, the therapist can write on her whiteboard or flip chart the following incom-plete sentences and ask the client to ‘fill in the blanks’ in order to identify his core beliefs about himself, others and the world (Greenberger and Padesky, 2016): • I am … powerless • People are … threatening • The world is … dangerous This simple technique can prove very helpful for clients with limited intellectu…」

## 步骤

1. 选定一个最近发生的、足够小的具体情境。
2. 按技术步骤练习，并记录情绪强度（0–10）与关键想法。
3. 写下练习后的新发现（哪怕很小）。
4. 若痛苦骤升，暂停并改用稳定化策略或寻求支持。

## 应用场景与问题

**常见困扰**

- 自动化思维、回避、焦虑抑郁相关模式

**相关情绪与症状**

- 焦虑、抑郁、羞耻、愤怒

**典型情境**

- 可具体想象的生活场景
