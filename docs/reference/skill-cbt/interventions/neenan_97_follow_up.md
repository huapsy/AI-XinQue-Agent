# 要点 97：FOLLOW- UP

## 简介

要点 97：FOLLOW- UP。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「How will the therapist know whether or not her clients are maintaining their therapeutic gains or determine the effective-ness of her treatment approach if she never sees them again once therapy ends? Follow- up appointments such as in 3, 6 and 12 months’ time can be offered to clients in order to monitor their progress as self- therapists – how firmly have they consolidated their gains from therapy? The therapist should allow sufficient time …」

## 步骤

1. 选定一个最近发生的、足够小的具体情境。
2. 按技术步骤练习，并记录情绪强度（0–10）与关键想法。
3. 写下练习后的新发现（哪怕很小）。
4. 若痛苦骤升，暂停并改用稳定化策略或寻求支持。

## 应用场景与问题

**常见困扰**

- 自动化思维、回避、焦虑抑郁相关模式

**相关情绪与症状**

- 焦虑、抑郁、羞耻、愤怒

**典型情境**

- 可具体想象的生活场景
