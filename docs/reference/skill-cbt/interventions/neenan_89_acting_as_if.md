# 要点 89：ACTING ‘AS IF’

## 简介

要点 89：ACTING ‘AS IF’。以下为基于 Neenan《100 key points》要义的**用户向自助说明**，非原文。 语境摘录（自动截取）：「This refers to a client acting as if his new belief was true even though he may not strongly believe it at the present time. Acting in support of a new belief strengthens it, which encourages further schema- consistent behaviour which then, in turn, further strengthens the belief – and so on until it’s internalized. The client is advised to pick a particular situation or arena in which to start acting ‘as if’ rather than attempt to promote his…」

## 步骤

1. 选定一个最近发生的、足够小的具体情境。
2. 按技术步骤练习，并记录情绪强度（0–10）与关键想法。
3. 写下练习后的新发现（哪怕很小）。
4. 若痛苦骤升，暂停并改用稳定化策略或寻求支持。

**常见困扰**

- 自动化思维、回避、焦虑抑郁相关模式

**相关情绪与症状**

- 焦虑、抑郁、羞耻、愤怒

**典型情境**

- 可具体想象的生活场景
